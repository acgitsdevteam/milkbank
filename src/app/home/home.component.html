Save a Preterm Baby's Life

There have been continuous efforts from all health organizations including WHO, UNICEF, WABA, IAP, BPNI, GoI and NNF in Promoting Mothers Milk as Primary Souce of Nutrition for prematurely born infants because of its gastrointestinal, immunological and developmental advantages. Protecting, promoting and supporting breastfeeding will save more lives of babies and children than any other single preventive intervention. Milk Banks are a Low Cost, High Impact Intervention towards saving preterm lives and ensuring Mother and Child Health. When Direct Mother's Own Milk (MOM) is not possible. Donor Human Milk (DHM) from Healthy Donor Mothers or Pasteurised Donor Human Milk (PDHM) from Mothers Milk Bank is the Next Best Choice.
